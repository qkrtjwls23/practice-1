BEST REDVELVET SCENES

An ever growing collection featuring some of the best scenes of RedVelvet

“Best RedVelet Scenes” is a collection of scenes I feel are some of the most affecting in RedVelet. Some may be simple black cat scares, others may be more subdued or nuanced. Many come from films that aren't necessarily “RedVelet” but have elements or threads of RedVelet, and all have the same general effect: unease, dread, fear, shock.
